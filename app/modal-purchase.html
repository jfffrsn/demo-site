Overview

The 31st Annual UCLA Intensive Course in Geriatric Medicine + Pharmacy and Board Review program emphasizes a functional assessment approach to comprehensive care of older adults and is directed toward health care professionals who care for older persons, and toward faculty in teaching programs in geriatrics and gerontology.

CME at Your Convenience

Without having to leave your home or office, 31st Annual UCLA Intensive Course in Geriatric Medicine + Pharmacy and Board Review allows you to learn from experts while earning CME credit. Available online or via MP3 CDs, this review allows you to learn on your own, according to your own schedule, or with your colleagues via our Additional User Package.
